**Problem:** Precision agriculture requires reliable crop detection and autonomous navigation 
over field environments — integrating perception, planning, and flight control across 
mechanical, software, and sensing subsystems.

**My contribution:** I led the perception system design and mechanical mounting integration 
for a quadcopter platform. This included designing and building the camera and sensor mounting 
hardware, integrating custom-trained object detection models for crop identification, and 
connecting the perception pipeline to the ROS/MAVROS flight control stack for autonomous 
navigation. I collaborated across mechanical, perception, and planning subteams to deliver 
an end-to-end autonomous pipeline.

**Tools:** ROS/MAVROS, Python, object detection (YOLO), mechanical design, Linux
